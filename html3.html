The Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, is a magnificent 1000-year-old Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, built by Chola King Rajaraja Chola I around 1010 AD, renowned for its Dravidian architecture, huge granite structure, towering vimana (temple tower), massive monolithic Nandi, intricate sculptures, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site



Lotus

The lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) is a sacred, aquatic perennial native to Asia and Australia, widely revered for its ability to rise untainted from murky water to bloom, symbolizing purity, rebirth, and enlightenment. As the national flower of India and Vietnam, it is central to Buddhism and Hinduism, representing spiritual detachment. Every part of the plant is edible, and its seeds can remain viable for centuries
